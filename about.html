About Us






Mission

Our mission is to support underprivileged children in grades K-8, located in the San Francisco Bay Area, by providing them with the finest STEAM education through after-school classes, summer programs, and more.






Our Team

She is a lifelong supporter of the arts and very passionate about supporting students of all socioeconomic backgrounds in their pathway to discovering the wonders of STEM through art. Want to Join Our Team?

Are you a highschooler at Mountain View who's passionate about STEAM and who enjoys working with kids? If you're interested, please look at the information below! We're always looking for new volunteers who are enthusiastic to teach!

Volunteer




We are always looking for teachers! No experience is necessary, but we are looking for students who have shown an interest in STEAM fields. If you are a highschooler at Mountain View High School and are interested in joining our team, please email our president Yana Kim at 100024370@mvla.net.
