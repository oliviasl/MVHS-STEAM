What is MVHS STEAM?



We support underprivileged children grades K-5 in the San Francisco Bay Area, by providing them with the finest STEAM education through after-school classes, summer programs, and more.

2020 Summer Program




We are super excited to be offering a 2020 summer program! We will be providing classes both online and in-person (choose your option when you register). The summer program is limited to incoming 4th and 5th grade students.



What do we do?

Our team creates programs, classes, and activities that allow students to have fun and be challenged at the same time. Each week, we will focus on one or more of the STEAM principles to create engaging hands-on activities for our students.
